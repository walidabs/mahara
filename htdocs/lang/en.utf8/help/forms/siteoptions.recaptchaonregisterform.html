reCAPTCHA on register form

If checked, people self-registering a new account will have to prove themselves human by passing a reCAPTCHA test. This should prevent spam accounts from being created by an automated script.

In order for this feature to work, you will also need to provide a reCAPTCHA site key and secret key. You can obtain these from the reCAPTCHA website, https://www.google.com/recaptcha.
