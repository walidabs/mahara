Edit page

This area shows a preview of what your page looks like. Changes are saved automatically. Drag 'Add a new block' onto the page to add one. You can then choose what type of block it will be. You can then also drag blocks around the page to change their position.

Designing your page layout enables you to personalise the way in which your resources and information can be displayed. For example if you wish to display only one of your journal entries, click on the "Journals" tab, then drag the "Journal entry" block into your page area.

As you build up the content in your page, you can check on how it will look by clicking on "Display page". Click the "Edit" button to continue editing.
